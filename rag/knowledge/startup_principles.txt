Customer validation is more important than product perfection.

Founders should interview potential customers before building a product.

A startup should focus on solving one painful problem clearly.

Early-stage startups should prioritize speed of learning over scaling.

Product-market fit happens when customers repeatedly use the product and recommend it to others.

Startup founders should avoid building features without validating demand.

Entrepreneurs should define a clear ideal customer profile before marketing.

Small consistent execution is more important than motivation alone.
